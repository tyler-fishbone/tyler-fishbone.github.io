The Catastrophe...

...happens hundreds of years in the future, when everything is better than everthing is now (and after everything got way, way worse than things are now), our society develops time travel. Further still, we develop it into a common household technology! It's mostly harmless. We even have a branch of law enforcement that deals specifially with time shenanigans!

Anyway, as with every great technology there are some who missuse it. Of all the things that are cured in the future, colorblindness was not one. There came one man whose name was lost to time but was one such affected by colorblindess. Instead of the usualy red and green loss, this fellow suffered blue and green loss. A genius by all regards, he specialized in most fields of physics, of which time was his strongest subject. He found a way to create a paradox powerful enough to collapse the spectrum of light of all photons in reality in order for light to not appear in a certain wavelength. He did this twice, successfully removing both green and blue from reality.

The Resolution...

...to this horrible event came from the brave scientists in our time. They were finally able to figure out how this man was able to do what he did, and even how to reverse what he had done! However, everything involved with time travel is incredibly delicate. While reversing the effects of one of his trips would be simple, trying to fix both would likely do more harm than good, so the infernal question was asked: which do we sacrifice? Which do we keep?

Thankfully, however, someone from the distant past had already posed this question to the world and found an answer! So, the color the world chose was reinstated back into the world, and we learned to live on without the other color.

Q. If you're from the future, don't you know which side wins?

A. Why yes! We know absolutely everything about what happens! We know which color is chosen and what life is like without the other color. To be honest, we were all a little shooken up to see this color for the first time ever!
